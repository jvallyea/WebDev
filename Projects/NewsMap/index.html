NewsMap

What does it do?

Visualize news in over 65 languages. Newsmap visualizes the tone of news articles published worldwide. We extract the overall tone, along with the geographical coordinates of the article to map the news media in a given time period. For business-minded users, we have integrated our platform with NASDAQ's Data-on-Demand API, to correlate the news activity of companies with stock performance. We hope that you will be able to use our platform to learn more about bias in the way news is presented across the world, and what we can do to better understand how to form a wholesome view of the world we live in. In the future, we hope to include new, cutting-edge features such as machine-learning predictive features to identify regions for corporate growth and risk and more efficient data compression to visualize longer periods of news data.

The Build

A Cross-Disciplinary Platform

Our core is in Python. The underlying news dataset that NewsMap builds upon is the GDELT Database by Google Jigsaw. We parse the compressed dataset by making SQL queries to extract user-specified information.

This raw information is then parsed using Python, and visualized using the Carto and MapBox API. Flask was used for front-end integration in Python. Sci-Kit Learn was used to build an underlying data set to train NewsMap to identify and predict regions of risk and growth.
